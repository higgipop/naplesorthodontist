Common Questions
Everything you need to know before your first appointment
How much do braces cost in Naples, FL?
Traditional braces in Naples typically run $3,500–$7,000. Invisalign is comparable or slightly higher. Most practices offer flexible payment plans and accept major insurance. See our full cost guide →
Is Invisalign covered by insurance?
Many orthodontic insurance plans cover Invisalign the same as traditional braces — usually 25–50% of costs up to the plan's lifetime maximum.
How long does orthodontic treatment take?
Most treatments take 12–24 months. Minor cases using clear aligners can be completed in as little as 6 months.
What's the best age to start orthodontic treatment?
The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a first evaluation by age 7. Orthodontic treatment is effective at any age — more adults are starting treatment than ever. Learn about kids orthodontics →
Do Naples orthodontists offer payment plans?
Yes — virtually all practices offer in-house financing and many accept CareCredit. Zero-interest plans for 12–24 months are common.
