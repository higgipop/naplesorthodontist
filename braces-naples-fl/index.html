Types of Braces Available in Naples

Naples orthodontists offer several types of braces to fit different needs, budgets, and lifestyle preferences. Here's how they compare:

Metal Braces

$3,500 – $6,500

Most affordable and effective. Works for all case types. Standard for kids and teens.

Ceramic Braces

$4,000 – $7,500

Tooth-colored brackets blend in. Slightly more fragile. Popular with adults and older teens.

Self-Ligating

$4,500 – $7,000

No elastic ties — fewer adjustments needed. Can reduce treatment time in some cases.

Lingual Braces

$8,000 – $12,000

Attached to the inside of teeth — completely hidden. Highest cost, harder to clean.

Most Naples patients choose metal or ceramic braces. Lingual braces are available from select providers in Collier County for patients who need a fully hidden option.

What to Expect During Braces Treatment

The typical braces process in Naples follows these stages:

  • Free consultation: X-rays, photos, bite assessment, and treatment plan
  • Bonding appointment: Brackets placed, wires threaded — typically 60–90 minutes
  • Adjustment visits: Every 6–8 weeks to tighten wires and check progress
  • Debanding: Brackets removed, teeth polished — usually 30–45 minutes
  • Retainers: Worn nightly after treatment to maintain results

Braces for Adults in Naples

Adult orthodontic treatment is increasingly common in Southwest Florida. Naples orthodontists regularly treat patients in their 30s, 40s, and beyond. Ceramic braces and Invisalign are the most popular adult options for their lower visibility. Insurance coverage for adults varies by plan — many Florida PPO plans include some orthodontic benefit at any age.

How to Choose a Braces Provider in Naples

When comparing orthodontists in Naples and Collier County, prioritize:

  • Board certification (American Board of Orthodontics)
  • In-office digital X-rays and 3D scanning (no messy impressions)
  • Clear fee structure with no hidden costs
  • In-house payment plans or CareCredit acceptance
  • Proximity to your home or workplace in Naples, Marco Island, Bonita Springs, or Estero
Braces FAQs for Naples Patients
What to know before your first orthodontic appointment
How much do braces cost in Naples, FL?
Metal braces in Naples typically cost $3,500–$6,500. Ceramic braces run $4,000–$7,500. Lingual (hidden) braces are the most expensive at $8,000–$12,000. Most practices offer payment plans.
How long do you have to wear braces?
Most patients wear braces for 18–24 months. Simpler cases may finish in 12 months; complex corrections can take up to 3 years. Your Naples orthodontist will give a personalized estimate after the initial exam.
Are ceramic braces better than metal?
Ceramic braces are less visible but more expensive and require more careful maintenance. Metal braces are more durable and typically less costly. Both achieve the same clinical result — the choice is mostly cosmetic and financial.
Can adults get braces in Naples?
Absolutely. Naples orthodontists treat patients of all ages. Many adults prefer ceramic or clear aligner options for a more discreet look. Adult treatment timelines are similar to teens.
