About NaplesOrthodontist.com

An independent directory helping Naples families compare top-rated orthodontists and connect for free consultations.

What We Do

NaplesOrthodontist.com is an independent referral directory. We help Naples, FL residents — and families in the surrounding Collier and South Lee County areas — research orthodontic treatment options, understand costs, and connect with top-rated local providers for a free consultation.

We are not an orthodontic practice. We do not provide clinical treatment or advice. We are a marketing and information service that connects patients with orthodontists.

What We Are — and What We're Not

We built this site to solve a simple problem: searching for an orthodontist in Naples returns a mix of ads, directories, and outdated review pages. We created a focused resource with real information about treatments, costs, and what to expect.

  • We are: An independent marketing and referral service
  • We are not: An orthodontic practice or healthcare provider
  • We are not: Affiliated with any specific practice or brand
  • We do not: Charge patients any fees — the service is free to you
  • We do not: Sell or share your contact information beyond the referral process

Privacy commitment: The information you submit through our contact form is used solely to connect you with an orthodontic practice. We do not send marketing emails, sell your information, or add you to mailing lists. Period.
